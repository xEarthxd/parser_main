    Fircroft is now enhancing their coverage to focus more on permanent Recruitment and Personnel Solution service. Now we're looking for Planning Engineer as detail below;

    Requirement;

    • Bachelor Degree in Mechanical Engineer or relevant
    • At least 10-15 years role project planner engineering in Brownfield (revamp) oil & gas in refinery plant or petrochemical industry plant
    • Can use Primavera software (P6) and good skill in excel, power point.
    • Good communication and coordination skill.
    • Working Team Work and Proactive working control subcontractor’s to ensure all activity can success as planning, familiar with company specification and HSE procedure.
    • Role & Responsibilities to provide progress report, tacking control sheet with part analysis and schedule planning and controller.
    • T/A work package, prepare presentation and reforecast schedule & progress reporting.

    Fircroft (Thailand) Limited

    Fircroft is one of the world's leading technical recruitment agencies, with a turnover of USD 1.5 billion (2013). With network of offices and joint operations supporting over 66 countries worldwide, Fircroft has global contracts with the world’s largest energy companies and some of the world's leading engineering companies.

    Fircroft has been placing people in specialist technical industries for over 44 years, focusing on mid to senior level engineers for contract and permanent roles worldwide.

    We currently have over 8,000 contractors placed in roles around the world and, as well as contractor recruitment, we also fill permanent job vacancies across our key sectors; Oil & Gas, Automotive, Power & Energy, Mining & Minerals and Engineering.

    Our success and reputation hinges on our people-centred approach. By ensuring our own people are focused, committed and approachable, we make sure the recruitment process is both seamless and enjoyable.

    When it comes to finding people the right job - we’re the right people for the job.
