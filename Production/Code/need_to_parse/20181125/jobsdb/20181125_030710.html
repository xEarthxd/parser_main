    Company Profile

    Executive recruitment company Monroe Consulting Group Thailand is recruiting on behalf of a leading marketing technology company. Our highly respected and innovative client is seeking an executive with at least 4 years’ experience in a consulting, analyst, or operations role at an online tech company, bank or management consulting firm for the job of Strategy and Performance Team Lead. The job is based in Bangkok, Thailand.

    Job Summary

    The Strategy and Performance Team Lead will be responsible for managing the social strategy and performance team, marquee advertiser strategy, trafficking, revenue growth, campaign strategies across your team, training and supporting in your market, product strategy involvement, best practice documentation and performance analysis for our advertisers running on social platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat & LinkedIn.

    The winning candidate will utilize highly developed management, strategic thinking, communication skills, a core understanding of online advertising and analytical skills for investigating campaign metrics as well as overall industry knowledge and strategic solutions to solve real world advertising problems. In this role, you will be responsible for managing Social Strategy & Performance managers, developing training programs and maintaining operational efficiencies.

    Job Responsibilities

    • Social team management. Manage a high-performing team across in your market. Set goals and objectives and mentor team members to deliver on company KPIs.
    • Training. Shape innovative internal training programs to educate the Strategy & Performance team on a growing set of solutions.
    • Strategic Leadership. Conduct quality control sessions with your team to compare current campaign execution strategies and iterate to find incremental improvements.
    • Become a Certified Advertising Professional. Become a certified expert on Facebook advertising by studying the Blueprint course and completing the Core + Buying certification exams.
    • Operational Support. Be the main point of contact to ensure operational queries involving Social campaigns are resolved in your market.
    • Workflow Management. Develop innovative workflow for our team to work with maximum efficiency across different functions such as reporting, optimization and communication.
    • Social Advertising Expertise. Regular research and education to fully understand and stay abreast of social media advertising ecosystems through individual research and publisher training.
    • Revenue Growth. Pinpoint opportunities for current advertiser campaign growth and improvement. Partner with the Client Services and Sales teams to identify and execute growth opportunities within existing advertisers.
    • Development Support. Drive best practice creation & implementation of campaign setup and optimization. Ensure the right level of alignment, consistency and flexibility in approach across the regional teams in an effort to scale impact while meeting advertiser goals.
    • Product Development Support. Coordinate with the product team to drive product improvements and solve needs. These improvements will include, but are not limited to, reporting, task automation and data implementation.

    Job Requirements

    • Degree
    • At least 4 years of experience in a consulting, analyst, or operations role at an online/tech company, bank or management consulting firm.
    • Intermediate to advanced working knowledge of Excel.
    • Exposure to and/or interest in online advertising pricing models (CPA, CPV) and advertising metrics (CVR, VTR).
    • Experience in campaign trafficking within a real-time bidding environment or auction system.
    • Experience in managing large advertisers across Facebook and have a successful work history in meeting deadlines and commitments.
    • Passion for Technology.
    • Good communication skills in English, both written & oral.
    • Must have strong interpersonal skills and the ability to establish, develop and maintain business relationships.
    • Ability to find solutions and deliver results within a rapid changing, entrepreneurial, technology-driven culture.
    • Strong ability to support department staff members, managers, and establish collaborative relationships with peers.
