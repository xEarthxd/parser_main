    Looking for a career not just a job? – Are you a young (21-28 years old), self-motivated, success driven, career orientated person with experience in Recruitment or have a background in Sales/Business Development? We would also consider smart, ambitious graduates with 6 months to 12 months working experience and fully train you on the job.

    Salary: Base Negotiable depending on experience + One of the best recruitment agency commission schemes in Bangkok (Junior Recruiter’s can earn between 50k-100k+ THB per month with commission) + health insurance + 15 day’s vacation days. 

    Location : Central Bangkok. World class offices at Exchange Tower, next to Asoke BTS and Sukhumvit MRT

    Recruiter Job Responsibilities:

    Recruiter – “360 Degree Recruitment”, including Business Development finding new clients and filling the vacancies where you advise both clients and candidates in the process (Full Training Given).

    Recruiter Job Duties:

    • Establish requirements & motivations of clients and candidates by asking questions and active listening.
    • Build applicant sources by researching and making contact with candidates building relationships via company database, social media, obtaining candidate referrals and advertising job positions.
    • Determine applicant requirements by studying job description and job qualifications.
    • Determine applicant qualifications by interviewing applicants; analyzing responses; comparing qualifications to job requirements.

    Recruiter Skills and Qualifications:

    • Thai Nationality
    • 6 months – 1 year experience in Recruitment or Sales/Business Development
    • Good speaking, listening and written English (You will be using English everyday)

    Personality Requirements

    • Ambitious and loves the idea of working in a fast growing ambitious Recruitment Start Up Company.
    • Likes to learn new things and develop yourself.
    • “People person” who likes to get to understand individuals who has a curious nature.
    • Career orientated looking for fast track promotions.
    • Smart Thinker.
    • Positive attitude.
    • Competitive, wants to do a great job, yet likes to have fun.
    • Self-Motivated.
    • Financially motivated.
    • Likes achieving targets

    Reasons to Work for us

    • Work in beautiful offices at Exchange Tower, less than 5 minute’s walk from  Asoke BTS and Sukhumvit MRT Station
    • Use your English skills everyday speaking with foreigners.
    • Good mix of Thai and European employees.
    • Very Generous Commission Scheme (You get very well financially rewarded for achieving good results).
    • Work for a fast growing and ambitious company who will help you be the best you can be.
    • You will be encouraged to work in your own style to make best use of your strengths.
    • “Hands on” on the job training from highly experienced and skilled Western Recruiters.
    • 15 days’ vacation days plus Thai Holidays.
    • Private health Insurance.
    • Company expenses fine dining at Bangkok’s best restaurants.
    • Fast Track Career progression with clear goals and career path.
    • Regular company holiday target for 5 star weekend breaks.
    • As well as excellent commission. We also pay weekly cash bonuses for hitting mini targets

    These are urgent requirements so if you are interested please send your resume (Ideally as a Word Document) ASAP for consideration via " APPLY NOW "

    RECRUITdee are a boutique Recruitment agency based in central Bangkok specialising in permanent mid to senior level candidates across the I.T, Finance & Consulting sectors in Thailand.

    By investing time into   training and developing smart intelligent motivated people, we offer our clients and candidates consultative recruitment solution to fit your current job/career needs. Due to this we can be confident that RECRUITdee can identify and approach candidates who have specific skills to match the exact requirements of each job position. By doing this RECRUITdee reliably identifies the best staff to fill your mid to senior level permanent job vacancies.

    For job seekers, we take the time out to fully understand your career situation and your motivations so we can consult with you and recommend job opportunities that match your career goals.

    RECRUITdee is part of RDA Group Recruitment Ltd.
